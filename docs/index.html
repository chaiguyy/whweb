Our Members

Our members consist of over 300 qualified students from elite universities such as Stanford, Princeton, Columbia, NYU, Berkeley, and others. Applicants contribute to a resume pool which we share with startups and innovative companies.

Our Mission

Founded in 2020, The Watering Hole is an inclusive space that connects the best and brightest university students along with the most innovative and fastest growing startups. Our personalized service is built upon our core values of accessibility, knowledge, and entrepreneurship. We believe students struggle to find job opportunities not because applicants aren’t qualified enough but because they aren’t seen in an easy and accessible way. The Watering Hole works to preserve strong connections between recruiters and students by hosting frequent small-scale career fairs throughout the school year.
